Green Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup pineapple frozen
  • ½ cup mango frozen
  • 1 banana

    • Mix all ingredients together in a blender.

    Acai bowl

    Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup blueberries
  • ½ cup strawberries
  • ¾ cup milk or juice
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 200 grams frozen acai puree

    • Mix all ingredients together in a blender.

    Chia pudding

    Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon honey

    • Pour ingredients into a jar and mix well. Let settle for 2-3 minutes then mix again very well until you see no clumping. Cover the jar and store in fridge overnight or for at least 2 hours.

    Banana pancakes

    Ingredients

  • 1 small ripe banana
  • 1 medium egg
  • 2 tbsp wholemeal self-raising flour

    • Mash banana in a medium bowl until smooth. Whisk in egg, then flour until smooth. Set aside for 5 minutes.ightly spray a medium non-stick frying pan with oil and heat over medium heat. Spoon 2 tablespoons batter into pan and spread out with the back of a spoon until 1cm thick.

    Enjoy!
