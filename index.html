Green Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup pineapple frozen
  • ½ cup mango frozen
  • 1 banana

    • Mix all ingredients together in a blender.

    Acai bowl

    Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup blueberries
  • ½ cup strawberries
  • ¾ cup milk or juice
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 200 grams frozen acai puree

    • Mix all ingredients together in a blender.

    Enjoy!
